Krispy Kreme Banana Pudding

This Krispy Kreme banana pudding makes for a great dessert

This indulgent dessert layers fluffy banana pudding with slices of fresh banana and pieces of soft, glazed Krispy Kreme doughnuts. Its a fun twist on the classic Southern favorite

Creamy, sweet, and packed with flavor, this treat is perfect for gatherings or whenever youre craving something rich and comforting. A guaranteed crowd-pleaser!

Ingredients

  • 2 (3.4 ounce) boxes instant French vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1 (14 ounce) can condensed milk
  • 16 ounces whipped topping
  • 12 donuts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 5 bananas, sliced, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Prepare pudding according to package directions and set aside
  2. Combine cream cheese and condensed milk in a separate bowl, and beat using a hand mixer until smooth
  3. Add pudding to the cream cheese mixture and stir until well combined
  4. Layer half of pudding mixture in a 9x13 dish, then add a layer of bananas and half of donuts. Pour remaining pudding mixture over bananas and donuts, then top with whipped topping and remaining donuts. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve
